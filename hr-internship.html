Program Overview

The HR Internship at Smart Versa is designed to provide hands-on exposure to real HR processes including recruitment, onboarding, employee engagement, and HR documentation.

What You’ll Learn

  • Recruitment & Talent Acquisition
  • Interview Scheduling & Screening
  • HR Policies & Documentation
  • Employee Engagement Practices
  • HR Operations & Compliance Basics

Internship Structure

  • Week 1: Introduction to HR & Recruitment
  • Week 2: Screening, Interviews & Hiring Process
  • Week 3: HR Operations & Employee Management
  • Week 4: HR Project & Case Study

Who Can Apply

  • Students from any stream
  • Freshers interested in HR
  • Aspiring HR professionals

Career Outcomes

After completing this internship, you can apply for roles such as:

  • HR Executive
  • Recruitment Coordinator
  • HR Operations Intern
  • Talent Acquisition Intern

Certification

On successful completion, you will receive an HR Internship Certificate from Smart Versa, which strengthens your resume and professional profile.

FAQs

Is this internship online?
Yes, it is completely online.

Do I need prior HR knowledge?
No, beginners are welcome.

Is this internship paid?
This is a skill-based unpaid internship.
