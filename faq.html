Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before joining Smart Versa

1. What is Smart Versa?

Smart Versa is an online skill development and internship platform that provides industry-oriented training, real projects, and certification.

2. Are the internships online or offline?

All Smart Versa internships are completely online and can be attended from anywhere.

3. Who can apply for these internships?

Students, freshers, graduates, and beginners from any stream can apply. No prior experience is required.

4. What is the duration of the internships?

Internship duration ranges between 4 to 8 weeks, depending on the selected program.

5. Will I get a certificate?

Yes. You will receive a verified internship certificate after successful completion of tasks and projects.

6. Are the internships paid?

These are skill-based learning internships designed for experience, exposure, and certification.

7. What kind of projects will I work on?

You will work on real-world projects related to your domain such as marketing campaigns, dashboards, websites, HR documents, or design tasks.

8. Will I get placement assistance?

Smart Versa provides career guidance, resume tips, and mentorship to help you prepare for job opportunities.

9. How do I apply?

You can apply by visiting the Contact / Apply Now page and submitting your details.

10. Is Smart Versa suitable for beginners?

Yes, all programs are beginner-friendly and designed with step-by-step guidance.
