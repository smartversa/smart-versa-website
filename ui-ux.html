Program Overview

The UI/UX Design Internship at Smart Versa helps you understand how real digital products are designed. You will learn user research, wireframing, prototyping, and interface design through practical tasks.

What You’ll Learn

  • UI vs UX Fundamentals
  • User Research & Personas
  • Wireframing & Prototyping
  • Design Tools (Figma, Canva)
  • Usability & Design Principles

Internship Structure

  • Week 1: UX Basics & User Research
  • Week 2: Wireframes & Layout Design
  • Week 3: UI Design & Prototyping
  • Week 4: Design Project & Presentation

Who Can Apply

  • Students & Beginners
  • Creative individuals
  • Anyone interested in design & user experience

Career Outcomes

After completing this internship, you can explore roles such as:

  • UI/UX Designer (Entry Level)
  • Product Design Intern
  • UX Research Assistant
  • Freelance UI Designer

Certification

On successful completion, you will receive a UI/UX Design Internship Certificate from Smart Versa, which strengthens your design portfolio and profile.

FAQs

Do I need design experience?
No, this internship is beginner-friendly.

Is this internship online?
Yes, it is completely online.

Will I create a design project?
Yes, you will work on a complete design case study.
